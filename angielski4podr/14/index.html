Zadanie 2

1. It means that they would move from place to place because they didn't want to use up all the resources of the land.

(Oznacza to, że przemieszczali się z miejsca do miejsca, ponieważ nie chcieli wyczerpać zasobów ziemi, na której żyli.)

2. The Aboriginal people didn't use to posses anything. They would share the material things, in contrast to modern people who care a lot about things they posses. 

(Aborygeni nie posiadali niczego. Dzielili się materialnymi rzeczami w przeciwieństwie do współczesnych ludzi, którzy przywiązują wielką wagę do posiadanych rzeczy.)

 

Examples of some of the rules from the text:

"These First Australians used to live a nomadic life." - to talk about a past action, we use used to. 

"They would move from place to place to avoid using up all the resources of the land." - to describe a past action (but not states), we can use would.

"Their lifestyle was completely different from ours." - to describe a past state, we use the past simple. 

"I really hate the fact that people are always talking about money and shopping." - to describe annoying present habits, we use the present continuous with always.

Zadanie 5

1. Tom always used taking the bus, but now he's got his driving licence.

Tom always used to take the bus, but now he's got his driving licence.

Uzasadnienie: po konstrukcji used to używamy bezokolicznika.

 

2. While Lily was living in Thailand, she had to get used to eat spicy food.

While Lily was living in Thailand, she had to get used to eating spicy food.

While Lily was living in Thailand, she had to get used to spicy food.

Uzasadnienie: po konstrukcji get used to używamy czasownika z końcówką -ing albo rzeczownika.

 

3. We're used to get up early for school now, but we still don't like it!

We're used to getting up early for school now, but we still don't like it!

Uzasadnienie: po konstrukcji get used to używamy czasownika z końcówką -ing albo rzeczownika.

 

4. Jeff's not at home now because he gets used to playing basketball on Fridays.

Jeff's not at home now because he plays basketball on Fridays.

Uzasadnienie: żeby opisać czyjś zwyczaj w teraźniejszości, nie używamy konstrukcji z used to albo get used to tylko czasu present simple.
