Zadanie 5

Mr Edwards called the help desk of an insurance company to report a theft burglary. He said that his house had been broken into the week night before . The assistant informed him that his annual premium was due in four weeks and his insurance plan was rather basic fairly comprehensive. She also informed Mr Edwards that in order to file a claim he needed to submit various documents. She asked him if he had filed a claim in the previous months, and he replied that he had hadn't. At the end, the assistant pointed out that it was wasn't guaranteed that Mr Edwards would receive reimbursement.
