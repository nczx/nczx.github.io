Zadanie 3

a.

Jacob buys cheap clothes in multinational chain stores.

He's got a lot of T-shirts, some of which haven't been worn for years.

b. 

Many clothing factories don't have strong building safety standards. 

The workers are in dangerous working conditions.

The workers are paid some of the lowest wages in the world.

The workers can be exposed to toxic or dangerous chemicals.

c.

The chemicals used in the production of clothes pollute the environment.

The plastic bags which we are given in most shops are a major cause of pollution. 

d.

Do research before you buy.

Ask yourself if you really need the item.

Look at the label and see where it's made. 

Check the company's environmental and labour policies.

Share the information you've found out with your friends on social media.
