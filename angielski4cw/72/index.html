Zadanie 3

Zdania z czasownikami przechodnimi, które łączą się z dwoma dopełnieniami mogą być zapisane w stronie biernej na dwa sposoby. 

 

1. The finished version of the film was shown to the producers.

The producers were shown the finished version of the film.

2. The tickets will be sent to us in an email.

We will by sent the tickets in an email.

3. The film was given bad reviews, but lots of people enjoyed it. 

Bad reviews were given to the film, but lots of people enjoyed it. 

4. The female actors were promised more money.

More money was promised to the female actors.
