Zadanie 3

Uzasadnienie: agree + bezokolicznik z to.

2. apologised me for not phoning the night before. 

Uzasadnienie: apologise + dopełnienie + przyimek + (not) -ing.

3. refused to buy a bungalow because it hadn't been very convenient for public transport. 

Uzasadnienie: refuse + bezokolicznik z to.

4. recommended that I stay at my parents' home and save up as much as possible each month.

Uzasadnienie: recommend + (that) + podmiot + bezokolicznik bez to. 

5. claimed that her parents had the best kept garden in the neighbourhood. 

Uzasadnienie: claim + that.
