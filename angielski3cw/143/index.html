Poniżej przedstawiona jest przykładowa odpowiedź do tego zadania. Każdy uczeń może rozwiązać je w inny sposób. Poniższe rozwiązanie traktuj jako wzór, na podstawie którego stworzysz własną odpowiedź.

 

Hi, Colin,

Thank you for your last email. Guess what! Last week our school organised a science exhibition. The event's main focus was how to improve indoor air quality.  

My class decided to prepare a presentation about chemicals that we might unintentionally inhale. We studied the ingredients of paints, candles and cleaning products, and prepared a list of the most harmful ones. It appears that some of them can cause serious health problems such as fatigue and allergies.

The exhibition was very interesting. The part I enjoyed most was the display of air-purifying plants prepared by class 2C. It is fascinating to see how we  can clean the air and make our flats less stuffy at the same time.

It was also inspiring to see what other students are interested in. Would you like to come and see the exhibition for yourself? It is on until the end of the month. Let me know what you think.

Take care!

XYZ

Thank you for your last email. Guess what happened last week! Our school organised an exhibition about the most important inventions of the 20th century.

My class decided to prepare a presentation that focused on the field of medicine. We did hours of online research and I learned a lot. For example, did you know that the polio vaccine was so effective it helped to completely eliminate the disease in Europe by 2002? 

The exhibition itself was very informative. The part I enjoyed most was the display of everyday objects we use to this day. We usually take things like the zip for granted. It made me appreciate them much more!

Taking part in events like this is a great way to learn new things while having fun. You can definitely remember everything better this way! 

Would you like to come and see the exhibition? It's on until the end of the month. Let me know what you think!

Take care,
