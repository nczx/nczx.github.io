Mam dla was coś wspaniałego: Pol'and'Rock! To największy festiwal muzyczny w Europie, na jaki można się dostać ZA DARMO! Wyobrażacie to sobie? Poleciłabym ten festiwal wszystkim fanom rocka i metalu! Post: (wpis)

I knew I had to see this concert as soon as I heard about it! Luxtorpeda, my favourite band, appeared at a popular open-air festival known as Pol'and'Rock Festival. The whole event took place in August 2022 in Czaplinek, Poland. It was a long-way drive from where I live, but it was definitely worth it.

The concert was fantastic. First of all, the entry was free and the sound quality was excellent. The most interesting part of the concert was when one of the artists jumped off the stage. I couldn't believe my eyes!

I enjoyed the festival a lot but there were lots of crowds. The mud was pretty annoying, too. Luckily, I didn't bring my best clothes because I knew better.

In all, the event is well worth seeing. I'd recommend it to anyone who's a fan of rock and metal bands but can't afford the tickets.


(Liczba słów: 149)

Festiwal bardzo mi się podobał, ale było dużo tłumów. Błoto też było dość denerwujące. Na szczęście nie przyniosłem/am moich najlepszych ubrań, ponieważ wiedziałem/am lepiej.

Forum post: (wpis na forum)

Polish music includes a wide variety of music types so everyone can find something interesting. Classic rock bands I'd recommend are: Republika, Myslovitz, Maanam, Coma, and Happysad. If you're a fan of rap and hip hop, you should definitely check out artists such as: Quebonafide, Taco Hemingway, Mata, ReTo, or K2. A good way to discover new Polish artists is to listen to radio stations that stream Polish music, for example RMF Polskie przeboje. Enjoy!
