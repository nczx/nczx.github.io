Zadanie 4

Przykładowe rozwiązanie:

a. Contractions (formy skrócone):

 - It's  (It + is)

 - don't  (do + not)

 - they'd  (they + had)

 - I'd  (I + would)

 - didn't  (did + not)

 - we've  (we + have)


b. Exclamation marks (zdania z wykrzyknikami):

 - I don't think so!

 - That means it was 7 pm before the vast majority of them were free!

 - So all of you guys working so hard - hang in there, summer will be here in just two months, and then we can sleep all day and stay up all night!

c. Questions (pytania):

 - And the owner of these things?

 - The purpose of a teen's life is to have a good time?

 - The survey also asked how many hours we had spent sleeping, and guess what?

 - Not much left, huh?

 - Time flies when you're having fun?

 - How would we know?

d. Addressing the reader (zwroty do czytelnika):

 - Imagine a teenager's room: books everywhere, a skateboard against a wall, music playing from the computer.

 - Take a look at the survey 'Your last school day' conducted in our school.

 - The survey also asked how many hours we had spent sleeping, and guess what?

 - But do the maths - ten hours for school plus ten hours of sleep.

 - So all of you guys working so hard - hang in there, summer will be here in just two months, and then we can sleep all day and stay up all night!


e. Idioms (idiomy):

 - It's NOT all fun and games (To nie tylko dobra zabawa)

 - have a good time (dobrze się bawić)

 - take a look (spojrzeć)

 - stay up (nie kłaść się do łóżka)

 - do the maths (policzcie to sobie)

 - Time flies when you're having fun (Czas leci szybko, kiedy dobrze się bawisz)

 - time flies (czas leci)

 - hang in there (trzymajcie się)


f. Colloquial vocabulary (potoczne słownictwo):

 - I don't think so! (Nie sądzę!)

 - a lot of (sporo)

 - Take a look (Spójrzcie)

 - Add to that (Do tego dochodzi)

 - I'd like to know (chciałabym siedzieć)

 - how we're supposed to (jak niby mamy)

 - guess what (zgadnijcie)

 - Not much left, huh? (Nie zostało za dużo, co?)

 - Maybe (Może)

 - How would we know? (Skąd mamy wiedzieć?)

 - when we've got school (kiedy mamy szkołę)

 - So (Więc)

 - stay up all night (nie kłaść się w nocy)
